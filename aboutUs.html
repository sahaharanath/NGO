Our Story

Santu Besra

Date of Birth - 10th March, 2023

On March 2nd, 2023, our community faced a devastating tragedy that would forever change us. Our dear friend, Santu Besra, was critically injured in a road accident, suffering severe brain damage that left him fighting for his life. For eight long days, we went through a heart-wrenching ordeal, hoping and praying for his recovery. However, fate had other plans, and on March 10th, 2023, Santu passed away, leaving an irreplaceable void in our hearts.

It was the 2nd of March, 2023, a day that would forever be etched in our memories. Our dear friend, Santu Besra, fell victim to a tragic road accident, suffering severe internal brain damage that robbed him of his senses.

He was rushed to a government hospital, but the inadequate services there only compounded the heartache. For hours, he lay senseless, waiting for proper treatment, but the limitations of the facility left him without the urgent care he desperately needed. Each passing moment felt like an eternity, as we helplessly watched his condition deteriorate before our eyes.

In the depths of the night, around 1-1:30 am on the 3rd of March, we made the agonizing decision to transfer Santu to a private nursing home, desperately seeking better treatment for our ailing friend. The doctors delivered the crushing blow â€“ Santu had slipped into a coma, unable to respond or even acknowledge our presence. Our hearts shattered into a million pieces, as we grappled with the harsh reality that we might never hear his voice again.

Santu's parents, burdened by economic constraints, could not bear the weight of mounting medical expenses alone. In a display of unwavering friendship and love, we rallied together, raising funds within our community and through social media platforms. The outpouring of support was overwhelming, as countless individuals opened their hearts and wallets to aid our cause. Yet, despite our fervent efforts and the generosity of so many, fate had other plans.

On the 10th of March, 2023, after an agonizing eight-day vigil, filled with prayers and hopes, Santu silently departed this world, leaving us with only cherished memories. The funds we had raised with such determination and love remained untouched, a bitter reminder of our loss and the cruelty of life's unpredictable nature.

As we mourned the loss of our dear friend, we found solace in the knowledge that our love for Santu had brought us together, united in a cause that transcended material possessions. His memory would forever serve as a beacon, guiding us towards a path of compassion, empathy, and unwavering support for those in need.

Our Mission

Inspired by Santu's story and the challenges we faced, our mission is to ensure that no one in our community faces such hardships alone. We strive to provide support, resources, and access to essential services for those in need, empowering them to overcome obstacles and lead dignified lives.

Support and Assistance

Offer comprehensive support and assistance to those in need, ensuring they have access to essential resources and services.

Education and Empowerment

Empower individuals through education, skill development, and vocational training, enabling them to break the cycle of poverty.

Compassion and Dignity

Approach our work with empathy, compassion, and respect, ensuring that each individual is treated with dignity and understanding.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a world where every individual has access to quality healthcare, education, and opportunities for personal growth and development. We envision a society where compassion and empathy are at the forefront, and no one is left behind.

Global Impact

We aim to have a global impact, reaching communities across the world and providing equal opportunities for all.

Collaboration and Partnerships

We believe in fostering strong collaborations and partnerships with organizations, communities, and individuals who share our vision.

Sustainable Solutions

Our initiatives are designed to provide sustainable solutions, enabling long-term positive change and lasting impact.
